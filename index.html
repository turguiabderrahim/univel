Whitepaper

Community Engagement

Univel is an interactive space intertwined with social life. Users can mingle, organize meetups, discover and join new communities, trade assets. Project owners can represent and promote their projects. It's a social space of constant interaction of Univel users with each other.

Univel builds games and social experiences that reach hundreds of thousands of visitors, where different communities can interact/spend time with each other and the upcoming projects can introduce themselves to other communities.

Virtual Reality

Experience the reality on virtual world. The only limit here is your imagination.

Users can dive into Univel using the Oculus Quest2 glasses with Oculus Link or Air Link (in Beta). Univel is compatible with other popular VR glasses but Oculus is recommended.
